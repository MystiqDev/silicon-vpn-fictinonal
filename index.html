FAQ

Quick answers before you choose your plan.

Is SiliconVPN real?

SiliconVPN is a portfolio concept used to showcase UI and frontend implementation.

Do you log data?

This demo follows a no-logs product direction, but no live VPN service is running.

Is SiliconVPN open source?

Not currently. A public repository may be shared in a future release of this project.

What devices are supported?

The concept is designed for desktop and mobile workflows, with responsive layouts across modern browsers.

Can I use this on public Wi-Fi?

That is the primary use case: secure your traffic when connected to shared or unknown networks.

Is there a free plan?

Yes. The demo pricing includes a free tier plus paid plans for higher usage needs.

How fast is setup?

Setup is designed to take just a few minutes with a minimal number of steps.
